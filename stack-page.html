Quick Reference
Function
Pipeline Management
Scheduledpipeline-agent
Scans Gmail and iMessage daily to surface new deals and move existing ones through CRM stages.
Ad Hocadd-to-crm
Turns any input — email, LinkedIn URL, screenshot — into a structured Notion opportunity in seconds.
Ad Hocneg1-scanner
Enriches LinkedIn profiles and logs promising founders before they've started a company.
Ad Hocadd-to-contacts
Saves a network contact to Notion without creating a full deal opportunity.
Ad Hocdocsend-to-pdf
Converts a DocSend pitch deck or data room into an archived PDF attached to the deal.
Ad Hocmaterials-handler
Downloads pitch decks and term sheets for a pipeline company, archives to Google Drive, and links the files to the deal page in Notion.
Function
Intro Management
Scheduledintro-agent
Scans Gmail and iMessage for new intro requests and logs qualified targets to Notion.
Scheduledintro-outreach-agent
Detects when outreach has been sent to a qualified intro target and advances them in the pipeline.
Scheduledintro-resolution-agent
Resolves outreach replies — moving contacts to Made, Declined, or NR based on their response.
Scheduledintro-draft-agent
Detects opt-in replies and creates a ready-to-send double opt-in intro email draft in Gmail.
Ad Hoclog-intro
Manually queues an intro to a specific founder without waiting for the next scheduled scan.
Function
Portfolio Management
Scheduledinvestor-update
Ingests founder update emails and logs them as structured notes linked to each portfolio company.
Function
Diligence Management
Scheduleddiligence-agent
Orchestrates expert feedback outreach and pass note drafting on a twice-daily automated schedule.
Scheduledfeedback-outreach-drafter
Drafts personalized emails to expert contacts requesting diligence feedback on a specific deal.
Scheduledfeedback-outreach-scanner
Detects expert replies and logs the feedback as structured notes in Notion.
Scheduledpass-note-drafter
Drafts a personalized investor pass email for every opportunity marked as a pass, ready for review.
Ad Hocfirst-pass-diligence
Runs a structured first-pass analysis on a deal, pulling all available context from Notion and producing a scored evaluation with a PDF and Signal alert.
Ad Hocupdate-diligence-priors
Ingests new call notes or materials since the last analysis and prepends a dated update section to the existing Notion diligence page.
Ad Hocfeedback-outreach-drafter (ad hoc)
Drafts a feedback outreach email to a specific expert named on the spot, outside the scheduled flow.
Ad Hocpre-mortem
Stress-tests a deal by imagining it has already failed and working backward to the most likely causes.
Ad Hocadd-conversation-to-notion
Archives any Claude conversation — analysis, notes, decisions — directly to the Notion knowledge base.
Function
Research Management
Scheduledresearch-agent
Scans the web daily for new investor letters and memos and delivers a digest via Signal.
Ad Hoclog-transcript-to-notion
Pulls a video transcript, extracts the key frameworks, and saves it as a structured note in Notion.
Ad Hocdeal-digest
Packages a deals digest from an external source into a structured Notion research note.
Ad Hoclog-investor-letter-to-notion
Saves an investor letter from any external firm to the Notion Notes database for future reference.
